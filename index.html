About

With extensive experience in mechanical maintenance and general repairs, our business offers high-quality handyman, repair, and maintenance services for both residential and commercial properties. Specializing in plumbing, electrical work, HVAC servicing, and equipment repairs, the business is equipped to handle a wide range of needs, from equipment servicing to commercial maintenance projects. With a commitment to reliability and craftsmanship, our service brings skilled, on-site solutions directly to clients, ensuring convenience and expert support wherever needed.

Our management principals bring extensive expertise in mechanical maintenance, including plumbing, welding, fabrication, and air compressor servicing. They specialize in project and building management, overseeing residential and commercial maintenance, and renovation projects. With a strong background in equipment servicing, team leadership, and customer service, they are also certified in forklift operation and mechanical maintenance, with advanced training from the Caribbean Maritime Institute and HEART.
